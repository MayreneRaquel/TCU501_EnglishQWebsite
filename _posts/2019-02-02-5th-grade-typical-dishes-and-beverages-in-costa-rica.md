<p style="text-align:center">In this post will present information about the 5th grade’s unit 4: Costa Rican Custom and Traditions. Topic: Typical dishes and beverages in Costa Rica. <br></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
<p style="text-align:center">1. <em>Gallo pinto</em>: Is a breakfast dish. The dish’s main ingredients are mixed together and paired with other ingredients to give it some spice, like red bell peppers, cilantro, onions and most importantly Lizano sauce.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"align":"center","width":378,"height":245} -->
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="aligncenter is-resized"><img src="https://www.govisitcostarica.com/images/photos/full-gallo-pinto-wooden-bowl.jpg" alt="" width="378" height="245"/></figure></div>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
<p style="text-align:center">2. <em>Casado</em>: A casado is a dish made up of many foods. If you order a casado at a restaurant in Costa Rica, you can expect to be served a plate that contains the following: rice, beans, salad, tortillas, fried platano maduro, and meat (beef, pork, chicken or fish). A casado typically also includes a fresh fruit juice.<br></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"align":"center","width":384,"height":256} -->
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="aligncenter is-resized"><img src="https://www.govisitcostarica.com/images/photos/full-whole-fish-casado-soda-ojochal.JPG" alt="" width="384" height="256"/></figure></div>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
<p style="text-align:center">3. <em>Tamales</em>: Tamales are a traditional Christmas specialty in Costa Rica, but many tipico restaurants serve them year round for the tourists. Costa Rican tamales are wrapped in banana leaves. <br></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"align":"center","width":398,"height":258} -->
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="aligncenter is-resized"><img src="https://cr.emedemujer.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/7/2015/12/tamal-2rt.jpg" alt="" width="398" height="258"/></figure></div>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
<p style="text-align:center">4. <em>Arroz con leche</em>: This is a dessert, the easiest way to describe arroz con leche is to say that it is rice mixed with milk. The recipe also calls for sugar, salt, lemon zest, and cinnamon sticks. <br></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"align":"center","width":370,"height":370} -->
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="aligncenter is-resized"><img src="https://www.govisitcostarica.com/images/photos/full-arroz-con-leche.jpg" alt="" width="370" height="370"/></figure></div>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
<p style="text-align:center">5. <em>Sopa negra</em>: Sopa Negra is a traditional soup of Costa Rica. Some of the main ingredients include black beans, onion, peppers, cilantro, tomatoes, and spices, along with Tabasco sauce.<br></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"align":"center","width":396,"height":264} -->
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="aligncenter is-resized"><img src="https://www.govisitcostarica.com/images/photos/full-sopa-negra.JPG" alt="" width="396" height="264"/></figure></div>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
<p style="text-align:center">6. <em>Olla de carne</em>: Costa Rica is known for fresh ingredients, and these are tapped into for the olla de carne recipe. These ingredients include potatoes, cassava, carrots, corn, plantains, and taro root. Combined with other vegetables, this stew is full of rich flavors.<br></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"align":"center","width":379,"height":285} -->
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="aligncenter is-resized"><img src="https://www.govisitcostarica.com/images/photos/full-olla-de-carne.jpg" alt="" width="379" height="285"/></figure></div>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
<p style="text-align:center">7. <em>Chifrijo</em>: The name is a combination of the two main ingredients: cracklings (chicharrones) and beans (frijoles). The dish is an amazing layer of ingredients that are served with fried tortilla chips, corn tortillas, or bread. The layered ingredients include the two main foods, along with rice, and either tomatoes or pico de gallo.<br></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"align":"center","width":422,"height":238} -->
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="aligncenter is-resized"><img src="https://www.govisitcostarica.com/images/photos/full-delicious-chifrijo.jpg" alt="" width="422" height="238"/></figure></div>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
<p style="text-align:center">8. <em>Patacones</em>: Patacones are deep fried deliciousness. It’s mainly served as a snack, and basically consists of flattening plantains and frying them. I like to have them finished off with a little bit of sea salt, and served pico de gallo.<br></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"align":"center","width":408,"height":306} -->
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="aligncenter is-resized"><img src="https://www.govisitcostarica.com/images/photos/full-patacones.jpg" alt="" width="408" height="306"/></figure></div>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
<p style="text-align:center">9. <em>Flan</em>: Flan is a sweet treat that mixes together milk, vanilla, sugar, and eggs, resulting in a delicious egg custard! Though cooked in a pan lined with caramel, it is chilled before it is served.<br></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"align":"center","width":408,"height":272} -->
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="aligncenter is-resized"><img src="https://www.govisitcostarica.com/images/photos/full-costa-rica-flan.jpg" alt="" width="408" height="272"/></figure></div>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
<p style="text-align:center">10. <em>Rice and beans</em>: This Costa Rican recipe consists of rice and beans cooked with coconut milk, accompanied by seasoned Caribbean-style chicken, patacones and salad.<br></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"align":"center","width":414,"height":310} -->
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="aligncenter is-resized"><img src="https://media-cdn.tripadvisor.com/media/photo-s/07/be/cc/ff/medio-rice-beans-con.jpg" alt="" width="414" height="310"/></figure></div>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
<p style="text-align:center">11. <em>Frescos</em>: They call them refrescos ("frescos" for short). These are a sort of fruit smoothies that are commonly made with water or milk. Due to the abundant variety of tropical fruits have been used to make fruit drinks.<br></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"align":"center","width":278,"height":212} -->
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="aligncenter is-resized"><img src="http://www.5aldia.org/datos/60/zumos_fruta_fresca1339.jpg" alt="" width="278" height="212"/></figure></div>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
<p style="text-align:center">12. <em>Coffee</em>: An aspect that has made Costa Rica famous around the world for its coffee. Coffee is the beverage that is obtained from roasted beans and the fruits of the coffee plant (coffee tree). It is a highly stimulating drink for its caffeine content.<br></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"align":"center","width":396,"height":223} -->
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="aligncenter is-resized"><img src="https://www.ecestaticos.com/imagestatic/clipping/710/748/71074837bc4aa3a4c71da9221ca466e8/las-conclusiones-del-mayor-estudio-sobre-el-cafe-mas-tazas-menos-muertes.jpg?mtime=1531409792" alt="" width="396" height="223"/></figure></div>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
<p style="text-align:center">13. <em>Aguadulce</em>: It is a water that has been sweetened with sugar cane or a kind of candy that is made of it.<br></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"align":"center","width":406,"height":305} -->
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="aligncenter is-resized"><img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/1c/Aguadulce.JPG" alt="" width="406" height="305"/></figure></div>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
<p style="text-align:center">14. <em>Horchata</em>: This kind of natural drink is made with a mixture of pulverized rice and corn. They also add some ground cinnamon and a bit of sugar. It is delicious and the best way to have it is extremely cold.<br></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"align":"center","width":389,"height":263} -->
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="aligncenter is-resized"><img src="http://bienstarclub.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/p55.jpg" alt="" width="389" height="263"/></figure></div>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
<p style="text-align:center">15. <em>Atol:</em>They also prepare hot beverages of different kinds that they call Atoles. A &nbsp;most popular ones are called Atol de Maizena, Atol de Piña, Atol de Naranja, Atol de Arroz, and Atol de Elote. They are all sweet and quite tasty. <br></p>
