<p>In this post you will find some important information about 6<sup>th</sup> grade’s unit 4. Topic: Different forms of entertainment in Costa Rica and English speaking countries. Please review it carefully and have fun learning!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Whether you are in Costa Rica or in a english speaking country, there are lots of activities you can do to entertain yourself, this includes both indoor and outdoor activities, and some of them could even become one of your favorite hobbies! Some examples of these activities are:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>1. &nbsp;&nbsp; Playing a musical instrument:</strong> Music is a form of art that most people love. You can learn to play instruments like the keyboard, to begin with, and then start learning how to play other instruments like a guitar or the drums!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>2. &nbsp;&nbsp; Photography:</strong> New generations are very gadget friendly, so photography or videography could be a great hobby for you.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"align":"center","width":197,"height":394} -->
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="aligncenter is-resized"><img src="https://mastergolflivestream.com/images/clipart-child-camera-7.png" alt="" width="197" height="394"/></figure></div>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>3. &nbsp;&nbsp; Reading:</strong> reading books can be a very entertaining activity for you. It helps to improve your language skills and vocabulary. And there are lots of different kinds of books you can read: action and adventure, science fiction, romance, comic books, mystery, fantasy, informational, and more!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>4. &nbsp;&nbsp; Cooking:</strong> learning to cook can be a very entertaining activity. There are millions of recipes out there, but you can start with something simple, for example scrambled eggs. Supervision from an adult is advised when you are starting.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>5. &nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Videogames:</strong> Videogames are a very popular way of entertainment. You can play on your own, or with your friends, it can be online or on the couch. There are thousands of video games available on consoles or PCs.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>6. &nbsp;&nbsp; Watching movies:</strong> movies are also a very important form of entertainment. You can go to the cinema with your friends and have an amazing time! You could also watch Netflix at home or movies on the TV.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>7. &nbsp;&nbsp; Walking, or Biking:</strong> Walking is a great exercise, you could take a walk around your neighborhood with your family or friends. But it you don’t like to walk you can use a bike too so you can have fun exploring around your town. It is a great way of entertainment that keeps you healthy.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<p><strong>8. &nbsp;&nbsp; Dancing:</strong> Dancing can be a great hobby too! You can learn different styles of dance forms like ballet, jazz, salsa, street dance, Indian dances, and more.</p>

<p><strong>9. &nbsp;&nbsp; Drawing:</strong> You can learn simple drawing and sketching techniques to draw whatever your mind fancies. Let your creativity flow!</p>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>10. &nbsp;&nbsp;Sports:</strong> Sports are one of the most important forms of entertainment, and they also help you maintain a healthy lifestyle. You can practice swimming, soccer, basketball, tennis, volleyball, gymnastics, baseball, and many more!</p>
